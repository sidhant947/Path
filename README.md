# Path

**Privacy-focused, lightweight, and fully open-source step tracking.**

Path is a modern pedometer designed for those who value their privacy and want a clean, no-nonsense way to track their daily activity. Built with Flutter, it operates entirely on-device, with zero dependencies on Google Play Services or external servers.


## Features

- **Privacy First**: No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your data never leaves your device.
- **Google-Free**: Works perfectly on "de-googled" Android devices. No reliance on Google Play Services for step counting or activity recognition.
- **Intelligent Tracking**: Custom-built motion classifier detects vehicle and bicycle movement to ensure your step count is accurate.
- **Battery Efficient**: Uses hardware sensors and optimized background processing to minimize battery drain.
- **Clean Aesthetic**: A beautiful, minimalist interface with a unique "lime fill" visualization of your daily progress.
- **Historical Insights**: View your activity trends over the last 14 days with easy-to-read charts.
